  • What?
    Gmathircle is a friendly math circle where girls from all over the world can meet together and not only discuss but also enjoy math. We will have meetings structured by qualified and dedicated staff and discussion on various topics .
  • When?
    Gmathircle's first session will be in June, specific date TBD.
  • Where?
    Online.
  • Who?
    Gmathircle is for girls in middle school and high school. It is best for such girls who are allowed use discord so they can enjoy Gmathircle's warm community outside of session times as well.
  • Why?
    Gmathircle aims to encourage girls to pursue an interest in math and bring girls together to not only learn but find the fun and beauty in math.
