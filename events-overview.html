Events Overview

Just a glimpse of GmathIRL's events!

Rabbits Hunt [problem hunt]
‍
If you're a girl currently in middle school or high school, please sign up for GmathIRL's problem hunt, Rabbits Hunt! Yes, there will be prizes, and unlike other contests, you can ask for hints from helpers.

GAME [math contest]
‍
If you're currently in middle school, please sign up for GmathIRL's contest, GAME! Yes, there will be prizes, and unlike other GmathIRL events, students of any gender are welcomed to participate.

Learn more...

Gmathircle [math circle]
‍‍
If you're a girl currently in middle school or high school, please register for GmathIRL's math circle, Gmathircle! Unlike the other two math events, Gmathircle will be year round with a discord server and biweekly sessions.
