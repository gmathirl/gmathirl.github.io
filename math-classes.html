current course [W21]

[GmathIRL W21]: AIME grinder
This four week course starting from Jan. 4th is for students who qualified for AIME from any of the four AMC10s in 2021 (AMC10, not AMC12). Each week will focus on these topics respectively: algebra, combinatorics, geometry, review+number theory.

finished course [F21]

[GmathIRL F21]: Gourging Geo and Catchy Calculus
This course teaches geometry and calculus from September to November. We have a small class size of 10-15 students and they discuss the given handouts and proof problems in the server. Please find our geometry handouts HERE and calculus handouts HERE.

What makes our course special?

The following is our course speciality and course schedule. Our course is completely based on a discord server, and it doesn't have solid times that students need to "go to class". Instead, we have handouts and problems, other class material, and office hours to make sure students understand and solve the problems given.
Note: Course material will be handed out through private discord channels and other miscellaneous activity will be held on a discord server whose invite will be sent to admitted students.

Highly qualified instructors

Our instructor is a MOPper and our TAs are well qualified. For more info, visit the "About Us" page.

Small Class Size

We are not looking for a huge reputation or a considerable number of students. Instead, we aim to form a close knit community of girls who can improve math together even after the course ends.

Specially for girls

Not many courses are specially for girls, but we thought this would help them become more confident in math and find their own talents in the field of competition math.
