Rabbits Hunt is an interesting problem hunt held in our GmathIRL server from February to April.

Participants will be asked to solve 15 problems in total which are divided into three sections- the first two of AMC level and the third around AIME level. We will give our participants one section at a time, which means they cannot get the next section unless they solve the previous section.

Since we only plan to recruit around 30 people for this problem hunt, most of our past participants will probably sign up for it. So if you are new to our community and want to fetch such an amazing chance, please sign up HERE.

What makes it special?

1. Prizes - We have special prizes designed by our president Serena instead of being sponsored from outside organizations
2. Style - We encourage participants to ask for help or discuss problems with others. In fact, they are allowed to ask for hints from helpers.
3. Form - Instead of a list of 20 or 30 problems, there are only 15 problems in total but each require participants to think hard and possibly gain insights from others.

GmathIRL has already successfully run two sessions, which means our community has already been established and we warmly welcome any girls to join it. Our instructor is a Mopper and manages to publish her own book back in December (Rabbit's Geometry), so you can really believe our qualifications. You can also freely discuss math problems on our server with fellow girls while enjoying casual chatting with Moppers, Sparc members or Ross members.
