GmathIRL

GmathIRL helps improve the minds of youths through math courses, competitions, clubs, problem hunts, math meets, and other fun events held mainly on discord servers.
We encourage everyone to attend these events and pursue an interest in mathematics!

a brief word about gmathirl

What We Do

GmathIRL helps improve the minds of youths through math courses and competitions held on two discord servers. If you are interested, check out our schedule and see "About Us" for the qualifications of our brilliant instructors.
Become staff

GmathIRL helps improve the minds of girls from grade 7 to 9 through math courses and competitions held on a discord server. If you are interested in it, check our schedule to see our course schedule and check "Our Team" for the qualifications of our brilliant instructors.

our courses

Our courses are designed for girls from grade 7 to 10. The current course spans four weeks and helps students grind problems of all subjects of AIME level, specially hand-chosen by our staff.

sign up for GAME
(math comp)

Please sign up for GAME, our math tournament for all middle schoolers! There is an individual round and a team round, and participants can form teams of at most 3 members.

Join our Community

Join our discord and subreddit for news on our Golden Arbitrary MathIRL Exploration (GAME), the winter course, and a chance to discuss math problems with other students of similar age who enjoy the art and beauty of math in a friendly and warm environment. Our discord server is open to everyone and those who signed up for our tournament will be able to unlock more channels!
